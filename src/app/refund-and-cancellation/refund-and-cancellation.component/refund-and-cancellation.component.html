Order Cancellation

At Sparkshopwear, we aim to process orders as quickly as possible. You can cancel your order under the following conditions:

  • Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement or before they are shipped, whichever is earlier.
  • Once the order has been dispatched, cancellation is no longer possible.
  • In case of a successful cancellation, the full amount will be refunded to your original payment method.

Return Eligibility

To ensure a fair process, items must meet the following criteria to be eligible for a return or exchange:

  • The return request must be initiated within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original condition.
  • All original tags, packaging, and invoices must be intact.

Important: Items must be unused and in original packaging for a successful return.

Non-Returnable Items

Please note that certain items are not eligible for return or exchange due to hygiene and safety reasons:

  • Personal care items and innerwear.
  • Items purchased during clearance sales or "Final Sale" events.
  • Items that show signs of wear or damage not reported at the time of delivery.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, it will undergo a quality check. If approved, the refund process will be initiated:

  • Prepaid Orders: Refund will be credited to the original payment source (Bank/Wallet/Card).
  • COD Orders: Refund will be provided as store credit or transferred to your bank account (Bank details required).
  • Shipping charges (if any) are non-refundable.
