Introduction

At Sparkshopwear, we are committed to delivering your orders quickly, safely, and efficiently. This Shipping & Delivery Policy outlines how we process, ship, and deliver your purchases.

Order Processing

  • All orders are processed within 1–2 business days after confirmation.
  • Orders are not processed on Sundays or public holidays.
  • Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation message with tracking details.

Shipping Timeline

We aim to deliver your products within the following timeframes:

  • Metro Cities: 3–5 business days
  • Other Locations: 5–7 business days

Please note that delivery timelines may vary depending on your location and courier availability.

Shipping Charges

  • We offer free shipping on selected orders (if applicable).
  • A standard shipping fee may be applied at checkout based on your location and order value.

Order Tracking

  • Once shipped, you will receive a tracking ID via email or SMS.
  • You can track your order in real-time through the provided link.

Important Note on Delivery Delays

While we strive to deliver on time, delays may occur due to weather conditions, logistics issues, or high order volume during sale seasons. We appreciate your patience during these times.

Delivery Attempts

Our courier partners will attempt delivery multiple times. If the delivery fails due to an incorrect address or unavailability, the order may be returned to us.

Incorrect Address

Please ensure that all shipping details are accurate. Sparkshopwear is not responsible for delivery issues caused by incorrect or incomplete addresses.

Damaged or Missing Items

If you receive a damaged package or an item is missing, please:

  • Contact us within 24–48 hours of delivery.
  • Share clear images of the package and product for quick resolution.
